PRICK Magazine is a new youth movement and

Dear Pricks. PRICK Magazine is a new media initiative and movement which is centred around the current political/economic/social and cultural climate in today’s Un-United Kingdom. We take no political affiliation and have writers and editors from all different backgrounds, creeds and political opinions. PRICK Magazine is going to serve as a common day critique of our country and the irrationalities and inconsistencies of our social and political landscape. PRICK aims to regress back into a form of free and universal media, without the fickleness and censorship of the online medium. This is why we have made the strict decision that all issues of PRICK will be a physical paper-back copy. Our main target audience is 14-24 and 25-60+ years old. We have an abundance of intellectual and investigative articles which will entice and engage the older reader, this is blended with satirical and non-cholent pieces serving to excite and entice the younger readers as well as offer a break of outrageous entertainment to the average news reader, shaking the conventions of the pompous and arrogant British Press. Another significant aspect of PRICK’s regression back to print media is to offer people an escape from the echo chambers of targeted video-news and advertising which riddles our online lives and social media platforms. PRICK has no advertising and doesn’t take your ‘precious’ cookies. We aim to cause a stir in the media world and to create a ruckus in the stiff and polarised nature of journalism we see in the UK today. We are the thorn in your side, the rudeness and directness which will make the nation murmur in disapproval under her breath.
